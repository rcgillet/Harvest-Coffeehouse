A MULTI-PURPOSE COFFEE SHOP

We are a full-service coffeehouse, used bookstore, live music venue, Wi-Fi hotspot, and local art gallery. We feature a wide variety of fresh-roasted flavored, blends, and origin coffees. Our coffee is available for purchase at our store in half lb. or full lb. sizes, and can be purchased whole bean, or we will be happy to grind it for you. We also carry a selection of our own special label pre-packaged coffees.

What we have to offer

We offer a wide variety of specialty coffee drinks such as Mochas, Lattes, Cappuccinos, Americanos, Smoothies, Frappe, Chai Tea, Loose leaf or bagged Tea, and many of our own custom creations such as our famous Candy Bar Mocha, Bavarian Breve, French Kiss and many, many more!

The Harvest Coffeehouse offers free Wi-Fi access to our customers. If you need a place just to stop and check your e-mail, or spend a few hours working on that online college course we are your place!

The Harvest Coffeehouse is a great place to plan your next event. Here you can fill out an application for your event and we will be in contact with you to help you set up your specific occassion.

We have a large bookshelf filled with used books available for purchase or simply to enjoy while sitting on one of our comfy couches sipping a freshly-made Cappuccino, Espresso, or hot tea.

The Harvest Coffeehouse is gaining a reputation as a place to see some of the best live music in the region. We regularly bring in some of the best talent from local artists, throughout the State of Michigan and beyond. We feature Acoustic, Jazz, Folk, Blues, Rock, and Christian music on most Friday and Saturday nights.

Soon we will have a shop set up that will allow you to buy your favorite merchandise that the Harvest Coffeehouse has to offer all from the comfort of your home.
